René Favaloro

A man who dedicated his life and his death to his work.

Dr. René Gerónimo Favaloro (July 12, 1923 – July 29, 2000) was an Argentine cardiac surgeon best known for his pioneering work on coronary artery bypass surgery.

Here's a time line of René Favaloro's life:

  • 1923: Dr. René Gerónimo Favaloro was born.
  • 1941: Completed his Bachelor’s degree in medicine.
  • 1949: Graduated from University of La Plata.
  • 1962: He moved to United States for further studies.
  • 1967: Performed the first ever successful coronary heart surgery.
  • 1970: Published Surgical Treatment on Coronary Arteriosclerosis.
  • 1972: Returned to homeland, Argentina.
  • 1975: Established Favaloro foundation.
  • 1980: Set up Basic Investigation Laboratory, now called Universidad Favaloro.
  • 2000: Committed suicide on the 29th of July after being driven to desperation owing to financial problems.
